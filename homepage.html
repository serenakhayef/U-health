About Us

Welcome to U Health

In the midst of a deadly pandemic, an economic downfall and an unsteady situation, the Lebanese population
could use some support. Fuel prices are on the rise, and transportation has become a luxury;
going around is risky as the virus is spreading quickly, and the situation is hard on everyone.
Hence why we decided to introduce UHealth: an online-based platform dedicated to give you the best healthcare
solutions, wherever you are. We provide services ranging from online doctor appointments,
disease predictions and health advices, and a 24/7 support center. Our team consists of Myriam our scrum master,
Serena our product owner, Michael and Laura our skilled testers, Carelle and Lynn our professional developers.



Our Services

At U Health, we offer many services ranging from doctor appointments and emergency care to a 24-hour call center ready to serve you wherever you are. Our qualified doctors are the best in the region and will make sure to take care of you and your loved ones.
